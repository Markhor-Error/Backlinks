How Blooket Works

Blooket is a teacher-led platform. Teachers create or import question sets and then choose a game mode. Students can then join the game using a code that the teacher provides. Once the game starts, students answer questions to earn points and progress through the game.

  • Bullseye: Students race to answer as many questions correctly as possible in a limited amount of time.
  • Race to the Top: Students compete to answer questions correctly and climb to the top of a leaderboard.
  • Team Race: Students work together in teams to answer questions correctly and earn points.
  • Escape Room: Students work together to solve puzzles and escape from a locked room.
  • Blooket Battle: Students compete to defeat other students' Blooks in a battle royale.
